The top 3 movies of all time

Raavanan

This movie has excellent visuals,music and acting.It taught me to see different perspectives of the characters.

Sita Ramam

Sita ramam is a work of art and defines pure love.The story is about the love story of a soldier and a princess.

Interstellar

One of the best creations of Christopher Nolan.The movie is filled with stunning visuals and spellbound science theories.
